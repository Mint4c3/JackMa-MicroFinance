Our Story

Why We Created This Cooperative

Financial literacy remains a critical competency for senior high school students under the ABM strand. Despite exposure to theoretical financial concepts, many students struggle with real-life money management.

To address this gap, we established Jack Ma Micro Finance a school-based laboratory cooperative designed to provide experiential financial learning through real transactions, profit-sharing, and cooperative governance.

Allowance Management
Address poor spending habits among students
Hands-On Learning
Real experience beyond classroom theory
Financial Discipline
Encourage disciplined saving & spending
Entrepreneurial Mindset
Promote cooperative participation & growth
"

The Process

How It Works

Our cooperative operates under a structured, transparent system that gives every member a real stake in its success.

1
Purchase Shares
Students buy shares to become official shareholders of the cooperative.
2
Coop Sells
We sell affordable school supplies, snacks, and services to fellow students.
3
Compute Profits
Profits are calculated transparently and reviewed by our auditor.
4
Distribute Earnings
Earnings are fairly split between shareholders and all members.
5
Report & Repeat
Financial reports and payouts are regularly presented to all members.
Profit Distribution Model
70% Shareholders Based on shares owned
30% All Members Equal share per member

This Structure Promotes

Full financial transparency
Member accountability
Incentivized investment growth
Real financial responsibility

How to Join the Cooperative

Membership is open to all ABM students willing to learn, invest, and engage responsibly.

1
Be an ABM Student Enrollment in the ABM strand is required for membership.
2
Register with Officers Sign up with the cooperative officers and complete your membership form.
3
Purchase at Least One Share Become a shareholder by acquiring your first share of the cooperative.
4
Participate Actively Engage in cooperative activities, meetings, and financial reporting sessions.
